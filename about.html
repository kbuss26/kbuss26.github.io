Who am I?

My name is Keifer Buss. I am 22 years old, and I am currently attending the University of Wyoming (UW). I expect to graduate in 2026 with concurrent Bachelor's degrees in Computer Science and Mathematics. My interests include logic and puzzle games, small coding projects, and mini-golf. Ultimately, I enjoy challenging myself to learn.

Accomplishments

At UW, I currently hold a 3.91 GPA, being on the Presidential Honor Roll several times. I am also Vice President of UW's Upsilon Pi Epsilon International Honor Society chapter.

Prior to attending UW, I attended the Frontiers of Science Institute program in 2021 at the University of Northern Colorado. This was a six-week program in which students performed collegiate work in STEM fields ranging from biology to programming to statistics. The program culminated in groups creating a research paper in their interests, where I worked with group members and an expert to analyze how COVID-19 impacted anxiety and social media use in adolescents as a pilot study.

During my senior year at Cody High School, I earned UW's Trustees' scholarship. This involves a two-stage application process: submitting the initial application and responding to both an online and in-person interview at the university. UW describes this award as the "premier scholarship for the best and brightest Wyoming high school seniors." Furthermore, I was the salutatorian of my class at Cody High School, and I was part of the school's National Honor Society chapter.
